## A. Versão principal (refinada)

```
Minimalist vector logo for "ARGOS", an audit and integrated-management-systems
platform. An abstract all-seeing eye built from a constellation of connected
nodes: six small circular nodes arranged symmetrically around one larger central
node, joined by thin straight monoline links, forming the silhouette of an eye.
A single bright accent dot is the pupil. Flat design, geometric, perfectly
symmetrical, even stroke weight, generous negative space, crisp vector edges.
Color palette: steel petrol-blue (#2C6E8E) links, midnight slate (#2B3F5C)
nodes, one turquoise glint (#1FA59E) on the pupil. Pure off-white background,
no shading, no gradient, no 3D, scalable icon, professional tech branding,
Behance / Dribbble presentation. Logo mark only, centered.
```

## Dicas de produção
- Gere **quadrado** para favicon/app-icon; teste o ícone reduzido a 16–24 px.
- Peça sempre uma versão **monoline single color** — garante uso em fundo claro, escuro e em carimbo/gravação.
- Evite sombra, brilho e 3D: a marca vive da precisão geométrica.
- Se a IA "encher" demais a malha, peça `fewer nodes, more negative space, simpler`.
